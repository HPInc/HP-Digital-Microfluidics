mpam.types.Ticks

class mpam.types.Ticks(mag: float, dim: Optional[Dimensionality[ND]] = None)

Bases: CountDim

A Quantity dimension for counting clock ticks

The units of this dimension are ticks and tick, so

2*ticks
1*tick

result in Ticks objects.

To get zero on this dimension, you can use one of

0*ticks
Ticks.ZERO

As this is a CountDim dimension, it can be freely added to or compared with a number.
