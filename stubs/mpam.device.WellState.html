mpam.device.WellState

class mpam.device.WellState(value)

Bases: enum.Enum

An enumeration of states a Well can be in. This is used as the target of a Well.TransitionTo Operation, most often as part of Drop.DispenseFrom or Drop.EnterWell (or the corresponding methods in Path)

Note that these are target states. While a Well is transitioning (via a WellMotion), it will not be in any of them.

Attributes

EXTRACTABLE

The contents are in positition to be removed by a Pipettor

READY

The Well is ready to move to a different WellState

DISPENSED

The Well has just dispensed a Drop

ABSORBED

The Well has just absorbed a Drop
