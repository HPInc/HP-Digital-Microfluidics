mpam.device.BoardComponent

class mpam.device.BoardComponent(board: mpam.device.Board)

Bases: object

A component of a Board. A BoardComponent implements the CommunicationScheduler protocol by delegating to its associated board. It can also be used to create a UserOperation by calling user_operation().
