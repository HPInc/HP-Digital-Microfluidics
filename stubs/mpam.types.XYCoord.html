mpam.types.XYCoord

class mpam.types.XYCoord(x: int, y: int)

Bases: object

An x-y coordinate pair.

The x and y attributes are also available as the col and row properties respectively.

  • XYCoord objects are Hashable.

  • Adding a tuple[int, int] produces another XYCoord.

  • XYCoord objects are equal if their coordinates are equal.
