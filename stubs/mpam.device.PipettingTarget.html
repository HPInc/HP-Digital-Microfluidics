mpam.device.PipettingTarget

class mpam.device.PipettingTarget

Bases: object

A place a Pipettor can add Liquid to or take it from.

A PipettingTarget establishes a protocol with respect to to its (optional) associated pipettor:

  • When the Pipettor is in place and ready to add or remove Liquid, it calls prepare_for_add() or prepare_for_remove(), which does not return until it is safe for the Pipettor to perform the action.

  • When the action is done, the Pipettor calls pipettor_added_() or pipettor_remove() to signal completion and inform the PipettingTarget of the Reagent, and Volume involved and whether that was the final such transfer to satisfy the request.

Note

PipettingTarget is an abstract class. Concrete subclasses must define contents, prepare_for_add(), prepare_for_remove(), pipettor_added(), and pipettor_removed(). If not overridden, pipettor will be None, which will likely cause things to complain if a transfer is requested.
