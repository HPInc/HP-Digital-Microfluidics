mpam.types.Orientation

class mpam.types.Orientation(value)

Bases: Enum

An enumeration describing the orientation of an array indexed by XYCoord objects.

This class arose because we needed to support devices whose internal coordinate system had y coordinates increasing from bottom to top and those that hand them increasing from top to bottom. These are encapsulated here by the values NORTH_POS_EAST_POS and NORTH_NEG_EAST_POS respectively. The other values are included for completeness, although I don’t expect to see them in practice.
