mpam.types.GridRegion

class mpam.types.GridRegion(lower_left: XYCoord, width: int, height: int, *, orientation: Orientation = Orientation.NORTH_POS_EAST_POS)

Bases: object

A rectangular region in a coordinate space

It is mostly used for checking whether an XYCoord is in the region and for enumerating coordinates within the region.

  • An XYCoord is in the region if its x and y values are between the corners of the region (inclusive).

  • Iterating through the region enumerates XYCoord objects from the lower left through the upper right in row-major order.
