mpam.device.transitions_from

mpam.device.transitions_from(sd: Mapping[tuple[mpam.device.WellState, mpam.device.WellState], Sequence[Sequence[int]]]) → Callable[[WellState, WellState], Iterator[tuple[bool, Sequence[int]]]]

Create a TransitionFunc from a WellOpSeqDict.

The yielded TransitionSteps will be based on the WellOpStepSeqs in sd. If either the source or target states are READY, (source, target) is looked up in sd. Otherwise, both (source, READY ) and ( READY , target) are looked up and the results are concatenated.

Parameters

sd – the WellOpSeqDict describing transitions.

Returns

an Iterator that yields TransitionSteps.

Raises

KeyError – if the Iterator cannot be constructed based on sd.
